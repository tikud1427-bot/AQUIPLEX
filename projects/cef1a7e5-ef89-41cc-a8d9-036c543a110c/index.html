About Us

Eshona is a peer-reviewed, open-access college research journal dedicated to the publication of high-quality, original research articles from undergraduate and graduate students.

Founded in 2021, Eshona is proudly led by Dr. Nripen Chandra Das, our Chief Editor, and a team of dedicated editors and reviewers.

Dr. Das is a renowned scholar and researcher in the field of [Editor's Field], bringing invaluable expertise and experience to Eshona.
